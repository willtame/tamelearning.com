Welcome to Tame Learning

We implement global strategies to ensure that language learners are supported in accessing the curriculum throughout their language-learning journey.

Guide differentiation in the classroom and display which language steps to focus on. Define language learners. This covers a broad spectrum, from children who are just beginning their English Language Learning journey, to children for whom the second language English is so proficient.

Note:A cross-curricular tool which helps practitioners observe, document, and accelerate the ways students learn in school. Measure the language development stages in speaking, listening, reading and writing.
