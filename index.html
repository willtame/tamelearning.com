The Assessment Framework is a cross-curricular tool which helps practitioners observe, document, and accelerate the ways students use language as a tool for learning in school.

The assessment measures development in speaking, listening, reading and writing. It is a clear and functional tool for formative and summative assessment.

Language Support

It can inform teachers what level of language support pupils need and to guide differentiation in the classroom and display which language steps to focus on.

We define language learners as any child for whom English is not their mother tongue. This covers a broad spectrum, from children who are just beginning their English Language Learning journey, to children for whom the second language English is so proficient.

We implement global strategies to ensure that language learners are supported in accessing the curriculum throughout their language-learning journey.
